Cockpit

The mission interface—not a chart template.

The AURELION Cockpit is where telemetry, structure, and timing converge into a single, operator‑grade view of the mission.

What the cockpit is

A unified interface that presents only what the operator needs to execute: regimes, structure, pressure, and mission context.

What the cockpit is not

It is not a signal feed, not a toy, and not a collection of indicators. It is a disciplined environment for decision‑making.

HUD & telemetry philosophy

The cockpit HUD is designed for clarity under pressure—minimal, legible, and structured around the operator loop: Observe, Orient, Execute, Debrief, Refine.

Mission‑grade UI

Every element in the cockpit exists to support execution. No visual noise. No distractions. Only mission‑critical information.

Screens & views

Future releases will introduce cockpit views, screenshots, and guided tours. v0.5 focuses on orientation—what the cockpit represents and how it fits into the system.
