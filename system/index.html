Timeframe Regime Engine

Directional Regime Engine

Direction is alignment, not prediction. The Directional Regime Engine frames whether the environment favors offense or defense, leaning in or standing down, acceleration or deceleration.

Structure Engine

The Structure Engine highlights the key structural zones that matter to disciplined operators—without exposing the underlying logic that generates them.

Pressure Engine

The Pressure Engine provides a non‑predictive read on where tension is building or releasing, so the operator can recognize when the environment is stable, stressed, or transitioning.

It does not tell you what will happen—only what state the environment is in.

Mission Timeline

The Mission Timeline gives a clean, chronological view of where the mission started, where it is now, and what phase it is entering. Not forecasting—orientation.

Mission Debrief

The Mission Debrief provides a structured, objective summary of what the environment did, how the operator responded, and what can be improved.

It is discipline in written form.
